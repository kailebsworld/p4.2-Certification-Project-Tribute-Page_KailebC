Cover art for Underdressed at the Symphony.

Early Life and Musical Beginnings: 1997–2016

  • June 25, 1997: Faye Webster is born in Atlanta, Georgia, growing up in a musical household that blends folk, country, and R&B influences.

  • High School Years: Attends the same high school as rapper Lil Yachty, though they weren’t close at the time. She later credits his early success with inspiring her to take music seriously.

  • 2013: Releases her self-titled debut album while still in high school, showcasing a folk and country-driven sound.

  • 2016: Briefly attends Belmont University in Nashville before dropping out to pursue music.

Faye Webster with Lil Yachty.

Finding Her Sound and Breakthrough: 2017–2019

  • 2017: Signs with Secretly Canadian, shifting her style to a unique blend of indie folk, soft rock, and R&B.

  • 2019: Releases Atlanta Millionaires Club, featuring Kingston and Room Temperature. The album earns critical acclaim.
Cover art for Atlanta Millionaires Club.

Expanding Her Artistic Reach: 2020–2022

  • 2021: Drops I Know I'm Funny haha, featuring Better Distractions and A Dream with a Baseball Player.

  • 2022: Releases Car Therapy Sessions, an orchestral reinterpretation of her songs.

  • Yo-Yo Skills: Gains attention for her yo-yo performances in live shows and videos.

Cover art for I Know I'm Funny haha.

Pop Culture Collaborations and Festivals: 2023–Present

  • 2023: Releases But Not Kiss, signaling an evolution in her sound.

  • Collaborates with LEGO Fortnite and The Sims.

  • 2024: Performs at Coachella and collaborates with the Minions franchise.
Faye Webster performing live at Coachella.
